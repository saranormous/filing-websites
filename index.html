Risk Factors 风险因素

1. Technology Breakthrough & Product Innovation Risk

The embodied intelligence large model ("brain") technology is still in R&D testing globally. If breakthroughs are not achieved, large-scale robot applications face uncertainty. Key risks include misjudging tech trends, failing to meet performance targets, and falling behind competitors.

2. Downstream Commercial Application Risk

Humanoid robot large-scale commercialization faces uncertainty. Technology maturity in areas like dexterous hand durability, embodied model generalization, and non-standard scene adaptation remains limited. Consumer market has not yet formed rigid demand.

3. International Trade Friction Risk

Overseas revenue exceeds 35% of total. US tariff policy volatility and potential export controls could impact both sales and ~20% of raw material imports sourced from abroad via domestic agents.

4. Competition Risk

Tesla (Optimus), Figure, Boston Dynamics, and numerous Chinese startups are accelerating entry. Competition is shifting from hardware to full-stack "perception-decision-execution" capabilities. Price competition may intensify.

5. Special Voting Rights Risk

Wang Xingxing controls 68.78% of voting power pre-IPO through Class A shares (10x voting). This may override minority shareholder interests on most ordinary and special resolutions.

6. High Growth Rate & Margin Sustainability Risk

Revenue CAGR exceeded 100% and gross margins rose to ~60%. Multiple factors including competition, tech changes, and cost pressures could slow growth or compress margins.
